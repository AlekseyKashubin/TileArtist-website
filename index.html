About Us

Tile Artist is a family owned and operated home improvment business. We have been serving the people of Whatcom County for over 20 years. Our goal is to find the right product, buget and design that leaves the coustomers happy and satisfied. Some of the services we provide are: Bathroom Remodeling, Countertop Installation, Custom Kitchen Cabinets, Flooring Installation, Home Remodeling, Kitchen Remodeling, Tile Installation, Custom Showers and much more.
